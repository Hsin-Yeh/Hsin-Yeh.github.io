AnaBHEL Description   Intro

Figure 1: Schematic layout of the AnaBHEL experiment. On the left is where we would receive the expected red shifted Hawking IR photon, and the partner mode would be on the other side which an XUV single photon detector is used. The signal photon pair are extracted from vacuum, therefore entangled and polarization anticorrelated.
